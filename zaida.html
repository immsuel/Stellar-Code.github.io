I picked these Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers because they remind me of Athena's wisdom, just like I see in you, clever and insightful. The mix of quinoa, feta, olives, and tomatoes not only makes a tasty dish but also represents the way you thoughtfully blend flavors in everything you do.

Ingredients:

Athena's Mediterranean Stuffed Peppers

4 large bell peppers

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove seeds and membranes.

In a large bowl, mix cooked quinoa, feta cheese, olives, tomatoes, olive oil, and herbs.

Stuff each bell pepper with the quinoa mixture.

Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Serve warm and garnish with additional herbs if desired.

1 cup cooked quinoa

1 cup feta cheese

1/2 cup chopped olives

2 tablespoons olive oil

Mediterranean herbs

Salt and pepper to taste

1 cup diced cherry tomatoes
