Frequenly asked questions

Frequenly asked questions

At stellarcode we receive many of the same questions. We therefore enjoy placing these frequently asked questions on our website for you. Is your question not here? Then please contact us.

Questions about websites

How long does it take to create a website?

Giving an indication for the development of a website is difficult. Each project varies logically. One may want a complicated customised website with various API links and functionalities, while another may want a fairly simple informative website. However, to give you an indication you can count on 3-5 weeks.

What does it cost to have a website made?

This is a question that is difficult to answer. Our prices usually start from € 2,000 for a simple website built with a builder. For a custom-made website it starts from € 5,000. Are you curious about your costs? Contact us and we will give you a more accurate estimate.

Can content be added to the website after delivery?

Yes, you can. However, it is not advisable to regularly change the content of landing pages. The search engines read your content and it often takes them a while to do so. Content that remains unchanged in Google for a long time scores better and ensures a higher ranking.

What are the results I can expect from search engine optimisation?

At stellarcode we look at the current results and ranking of a website before we start a collaboration. We make several printouts of the website. That way we can see exactly what the starting point is. After we’ve started working for you, we make monthly progress reports. We can never give an exact indication of results. However, we can show you the results that we have achieved in the past.

Do we also offer website maintenance?

Yes, we do. However, we only maintain websites that we have developed ourselves. We have several possibilities for this. Feel free to contact us to learn more about how we can maintain your website.

What will my website be built with?

We build all websites to measure and then integrate them into a Management System (CMS). There are many different possibilities when it comes to the CMS in which a website is managed. We have chosen to do this in WordPress in most cases, because it is easy to use and offers unrivalled stability and reliability. Read more about web development.

What does the keyword research entail?

Before we start developing the web design, it is important to have a clear idea of the search terms you want to be found on. During the keyword research we look at related search terms and the competition. For example, what is the search volume and the KD percentage (keyword difficulty)? On the basis of these results, we give advice on which keywords the organisation should focus.

Questions about working with us

Do we also offer support?

Yes, we do. We offer various support options. Please contact us to find out what the possibilities are.

Can stellarcode also host my website? And what are the advantages?

Yes, we can. By outsourcing your hosting to us, you just know you won’t run into any problems. For more information on hosting, see the hosting page.

Is there a monthly charge for a website built by stellarcode?

No, there are no compulsory monthly costs associated with a website built by stellarcode. But it is advisable to have the website maintained and hosted by stellarcode. You’re then assured of no problems with your website.

Does stellarcode offer guarantee on the delivered website?

Yes, we offer a full guarantee when you choose to purchase a support contract. This guarantee is only valid if it is clear that these errors are caused by stellarcode.

Is there a feedback round included if I have a website developed by stellarcode?

Yes, we include one feedback round per sprint as standard. We have noticed that this feedback round is hardly ever used. If, for any reason, you want more feedback rounds, we can always arrange that.

Questions about branding

Can I also have a logo or corporate identity developed?

No, the whole branding process is necessary to achieve a catchy corporate identity and logo that fits the organisation.

What are the costs of having a new house style developed?

This is difficult to estimate and depends on how much time is needed to get to know the organisation. Obviously, it also depends on the size of the organisation. How many interviews are needed, for example? And how long do we need to convert this information into a strategy and positioning? This can vary from €2,000 to €25,000.

How long do you spend developing a new corporate identity?

That too is difficult to say. It could be two weeks, but it could also be several months. This depends on the size of the organisation.
