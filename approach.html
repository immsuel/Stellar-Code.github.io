Communication and transparency are key

Our unique approach

Together we like to work on something beautiful. We do this from the perspective that it must be beautiful (User Interface) and user-friendly (User Experience). We work daily with strategists, designers and developers to make organisations visible online.

The Kick-off

If you are interested in a possible cooperation and would like to have a website designed, built or optimised, we would like to invite you to a free online consultation no strings attached.

We will see if it clicks and if we are the right partner for your needs. During this meeting, we will look at how we can best reach your target group and how we can translate your vision to the website. Does it click? Then after the meeting we will make you a suitable proposal based on the design and functionality of the website.

01

Branding & strategy

After the kick-off, we get to work together. Our UX specialists start by defining the project. Here, we do research and make various analyses. We determine the brand identity and characterise your brand. Then we apply a dose of psychology to make certain choices in the appearance. We also do an extensive keyword research to make sure your website scores in the search engine.

02

Webdesign

Next, our UX designers get to work. They start with setting up the site structure, after which we start wireframing: digital line sketching. They look at what the website will look like on a functional level. After several sessions in which we process your feedback, the wireframing is perfected and we start with the web design. Our designers start working on the user interface. They look at the different functionalities and create a catchy look.

03

Development

Once the web design is set, we start developing. During the development phase, the website comes to life. Depending on the size of the project, we agree on rhythms. This Agile way of working ensures not only involvement but also no surprises at the end of the ride. Our developers build the website to the highest quality standards. Next, we put the website in a test environment, where we perform executive tests. This way, you are assured of a perfectly functioning web application.

04

Launching

When our developers have finished the development phase, we invite you to a meeting where we present the website or web application and tell you exactly what we did and why. Of course, we already know what the end result will look like. We launch the website together and evaluate the cooperation.

05

Optimisation

In the next phase, we will discuss your website further and see where it can be improved. In the further development phase, we optimise your web pages or add extra features. For example, we will work to get your website ranked higher in the search engine.
